About

Systems thinker. Creative lead. Studio founder.

I design the invisible architecture of games: the economies, loops, and systems that keep players coming back. I've built a studio from nothing, directed open-world games, and designed social mechanics that turn casual players into real communities.

My years in product and UX design sharpened my prototyping habits and my knack for thinking in several personas at once, and that carries straight over into complex game feature work. Claude AI is part of my daily workflow too, for everything from quick GDD drafts to system docs and design iteration.
